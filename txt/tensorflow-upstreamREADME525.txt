<h1>TensorFlow Lite</h1>
<p>TensorFlow Lite is TensorFlow's lightweight solution for mobile and embedded
devices. It enables low-latency inference of on-device machine learning models
with a small binary size and fast performance supporting hardware acceleration.</p>
<p>See the documentation: https://www.tensorflow.org/lite/
Documentation edits can be made here: <a href="./g3doc/">tensorflow/lite/g3doc</a></p>
