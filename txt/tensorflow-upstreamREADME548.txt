<h1>Build TensorFlow Lite with Makefile</h1>
<p><strong>WARNING: Using Makefile to build TensorFlow Lite is deprecated since
Aug 2021.</strong></p>
<p>Please use CMake or Bazel instead. Please refer to the
<a href="https://www.tensorflow.org/lite/guide/build_cmake">Build TensorFlow Lite with CMake</a>
and <a href="https://www.tensorflow.org/lite/guide/build_arm">Build TensorFlow Lite for ARM boards</a>
for the details.</p>
