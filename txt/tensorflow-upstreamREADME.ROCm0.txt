<h1>Tensorflow ROCm port</h1>
<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>This repository hosts the port of <a href="https://github.com/tensorflow/tensorflow">Tensorflow</a> on ROCm platform. It uses various technologies on ROCm platform such as HIP and MIOpen. For details on HIP, please refer <a href="https://github.com/GPUOpen-ProfessionalCompute-Tools/HIP">here</a>. Optimized DNN library calls (via <a href="https://github.com/ROCmSoftwarePlatform/MIOpen">MIOpen</a>) are also supported within this codebase.</p>
