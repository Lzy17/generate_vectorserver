<h1>ARM GCC toolchains</h1>
<p>This repository contains Bazel C++ toolchain configurations for ARM GCC. The
following toolchains are configured:</p>
<p>source:
https://developer.arm.com/tools-and-software/open-source-software/developer-tools/gnu-toolchain/gnu-a/downloads -
gcc 8.3 - glibc 2.28</p>
<p>target cpu: aarch64, armhf</p>
